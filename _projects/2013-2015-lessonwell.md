Lessonwell started out as a personal project for my own Calculus class. I wanted a content management platform based on Markdown and MathJax that I could use to quickly prototype lessons without navigating through an insanely complicated education CMS (which is basically every education CMS).

![lessonwell](/images/lessonwell.png)

Lessonwell eventually turned into my first business venture, where I also learned true fullstack software development; it is also what eventually led me into a career in software. I wrote a bit about some lessons learned during the development of lessonwell in the blog post: [My First Big Software Startup Failure][1].
