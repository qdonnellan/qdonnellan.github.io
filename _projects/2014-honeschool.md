![honeschool](/images/honeschool.png)

Honeschool grew out of an after-school program I developed over several years while still a teacher at Keller ISD. Our plan was to develop a skills-based curriculum to area students with the goal of presenting our students to local employers and eventually universities each with a portfolio of work that demonstrated competencies beyond what secondary education credentials could represent. Our first program centered around robotics and programming.

I eventually had to step aside as founder as time with my newborn child and newly acquired remote engineering position. 

While the program was still active we were very pleased with the engagement and success of our students. We taught them Ruby using the Sphero robot and the [artoo.io][1] platform.
